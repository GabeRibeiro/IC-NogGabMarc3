Luís Vaz de Camões was a Portuguese national poet, considered one of the greatest figures in Lusophone literature and one of the great poets of the Western tradition. Little is known for sure about his life. Apparently he was born in Lisbon, from a family of the gentry.
Fernando António Nogueira Pessoa was a Portuguese poet, philosopher, playwright, essayist, translator, publicist, astrologer, inventor, entrepreneur, commercial correspondent, literary critic and political commentator. Fernando Pessoa is the most universal Portuguese poet.
José de Sousa Saramago was a Portuguese writer. Awarded with the Nobel Prize for Literature in 1998. In 1995, he also won the Camões Prize, the most important literary prize in the Portuguese language. Saramago was considered responsible for the effective international recognition of prose in Portuguese.
